Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go.

If you would like to permanently delete your Aquaria AI account and all associated data, you can do so in one of two ways:

Option 1: From the App

Open the Aquaria AI app, tap the account icon in the top right, go to Profile, and tap Close Account at the bottom of the screen. Your account will be deactivated and your data will be scheduled for permanent deletion.

Option 2: By Email

Send an email to info@aquaria-ai.com from the email address registered to your account with the subject line "DELETE". We will verify your identity and process your request.

Send Deletion Request

What happens when you delete your account:

  • Your account is immediately deactivated and you will be signed out.
  • All personal data — including tank profiles, care logs, journal entries, photographs, and community posts — will be permanently deleted within 12 months of your request, as described in our Privacy Policy.
  • Content you shared publicly in the community feed may remain visible until deletion is complete.
  • This action cannot be undone.

If you have any questions, contact us at info@aquaria-ai.com.
