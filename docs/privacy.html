  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of Aquaria AI, our users, or others; and
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity.

2.5 AI Model Training — Opt-Out

The App currently relies on third-party AI model providers to process your animal care data and generate recommendations. Your animal care information — including species data, tank parameters, care logs, and related inputs — is transmitted to those providers for this purpose.

Uploaded images are not currently transmitted to any third-party AI provider. We reserve the right to extend third-party AI processing to uploaded images in the future, in which case this Policy will be updated to reflect that change prior to such processing occurring. 4. Uploaded Images — Detailed Practices

4.1 Private Uploads for AI Analysis

Images you upload privately for the purpose of receiving AI-generated analysis and recommendations ("Private Uploads") are:

  • Stored and processed on our own servers to generate AI recommendations. Uploaded images are not currently transmitted to any third-party AI provider for processing;
  • Stored on our secure servers for the duration of your account;
  • Not displayed publicly on the App or shared with other users; and
  • Not used for promotional or marketing purposes without your separate consent.

4.2 Publicly Shared Images

If and when social or community features are available, images you choose to share publicly through those features ("Public Uploads") are subject to a broader license as described in the Terms and Conditions, including potential use in Aquaria AI promotional and marketing materials. You should not publicly share any image that you do not wish to potentially appear in marketing contexts.

4.3 Retention and Deletion of Images

Private Uploads are retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account closure, we will delete your uploaded images within twelve (12) months, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention or legal proceedings. If you submit a formal deletion request for specific uploaded images, we will complete that deletion within forty-five (45) days of verifying your request. You may submit a deletion request at any time by contacting info@aquaria-ai.com.

4.4 Biometric and Sensitive Visual Data

We do not intentionally collect biometric data from users. If you upload images that incidentally contain images of people, we do not use facial recognition technology on such images and do not extract biometric identifiers. You should avoid uploading images containing identifiable individuals without their consent.

5. Promotional Use of Images

As described in our Terms and Conditions, Aquaria AI may use photographs and images you publicly share through the App in promotional and marketing materials. This may include use on our website, social media channels, advertising campaigns, press releases, and other marketing contexts.

Private Uploads used solely for AI analysis are not used for promotional purposes. The distinction between private and public uploads is governed by how you choose to share content within the App.

If you believe an image of yours has been used in a manner inconsistent with this policy, please contact us at info@aquaria-ai.com and we will promptly review your request.

6. Push Notifications and Communication

6.1 Push Notifications

With your permission, we collect your device's push notification token to deliver care reminders, alerts, and App updates. You may disable push notifications at any time through your device's operating system settings. Disabling notifications may affect the functionality of scheduled care features within the App.

6.2 Email Communications

If you provide your email address, we may use it to send transactional communications (account confirmations, password resets, support responses) and, where you have opted in, promotional emails. You may opt out of promotional emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any promotional email or by contacting us directly. Transactional emails are not subject to opt-out as they are necessary to operate your account.

6.3 In-App Notifications

We may send notifications within the App regarding App updates, new features, policy changes, and care-related information. These are part of the core App experience and cannot be disabled without ceasing use of the App.

7. Location Data

We may request access to your device's approximate location to provide climate- and region-based aquatic care recommendations (for example, adjusting heating recommendations based on ambient temperatures in your region). We do not collect precise GPS location data.

Location access is optional. If you decline, all App features remain available, though certain location-based recommendations may be less precise. You may revoke location permissions at any time through your device's settings. We do not share your location data with advertisers or third-party marketing platforms.

8. Social and Community Features — Data Practices

NOTE: The provisions of this Section apply if and when Aquaria AI makes social or community features available within the App. These terms are included prospectively and shall become operative upon the activation of such features.

8.1 Visibility of Your Information

To the extent social or community features are available, certain information you provide may be visible to other users of the App, including:

  • Your display name;
  • Content you post to public community feeds or forums;
  • Images you share publicly; and
  • Comments or responses you make on other users' posts.

Information you choose to keep private — such as your animal care logs, Private Uploads, and account email — will not be visible to other users. You are responsible for the information you choose to share publicly within Social Features.

8.2 Direct Messages

If direct messaging features are available, messages you send to other users are stored on our servers to facilitate delivery. Aquaria AI personnel do not routinely access the content of private messages, but may do so in connection with: (a) a valid legal request; (b) investigation of a reported violation of our Terms and Conditions; or (c) detection of illegal activity or imminent safety threats. Message content may be retained for a period after deletion for legal compliance and safety purposes.

8.3 Data Shared With Other Users

You acknowledge that information shared with other users through Social Features — including messages, comments, and publicly posted images — may be copied, shared, or retained by those users outside of Aquaria AI's control. Aquaria AI cannot retrieve or delete content that has been copied or re-shared by other users.

8.4 Blocking and Privacy Controls

Where Social Features are available, the App will provide tools to block or restrict interactions with other users. Blocking a user prevents them from seeing your future public posts and sending you direct messages, but does not remove content you have previously posted publicly or retroactively delete messages already delivered.

8.5 Account Deletion and Social Data

Upon account closure, your profile and privately held data will be deleted within twelve (12) months. However, content you have publicly posted through Social Features — such as comments or shared images — may remain visible to other users. If you wish to remove specific publicly posted content prior to account closure, you must do so manually before requesting closure.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the App's services. Specific retention periods are as follows:

  • Account and profile data: Retained for the life of your account, and deleted within 12 months of account closure.
  • Animal care logs and data: Retained for the life of your account, and deleted within 12 months of account closure.
  • Private Uploads: Retained for the life of your account, and deleted within 12 months of account closure.
  • Public Uploads and social content: Retained until manually deleted by you or upon account deletion, subject to the caveats in Section 8.5.
  • Usage and analytics data: Retained in anonymized or aggregated form for up to three (3) years for product improvement purposes.
  • Legal and compliance records: Retained as required by applicable law, which may extend beyond account closure.

We may retain certain data beyond these periods where necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, or protect against fraud.

We implement industry-standard technical, administrative, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using Transport Layer Security (TLS);
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest;
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis;
  • Regular security assessments and monitoring; and
  • Incident response procedures for potential data breaches.

However, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information, and we encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and to notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you as required by applicable law.
